A station that plays the music you know and love

107.3 Total FM is a low power, not-for-profit FM radio station based in Dunedin, New Zealand.

We’re not a networked radio brand that comes from somewhere else in the country. Dunedin is our home. We broadcast 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and can be heard across most of the city.

If we had a middle name on our birth certificate it would be variety – the artists we play include: Simple Minds, Coldplay, Tears for Fears, Pink, Queen, David Bowie, Taylor Swift, INXS, Peter Gabriel and more…
